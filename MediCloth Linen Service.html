Our Story

Built for Radiology. Focused on Compliance.

Who We Are

A Linen Company That Understands Imaging

MediCloth Linen Service was founded with a single focus: serve radiology and diagnostic imaging centers with the right linen at the right price — not oversell them on heavy BBP-rated products designed for operating rooms.

We recognized that most imaging centers were being charged for full blood-borne pathogen linen protocols they didn't need. Non-invasive procedures don't require surgical-grade linen. We built a program around that simple truth.

Today we serve independent imaging centers, radiology groups, and outpatient imaging suites across Los Angeles, Orange County, San Diego and Riverside — and we're expanding. Our clients save an average of 15–20% on linen costs while meeting every applicable compliance standard.
